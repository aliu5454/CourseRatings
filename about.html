About Us

We're UBC students, just like you.

After 4 years of researching blogs, asking classmates and friends, and looking through course websites only to find limited information on courses, we decided that it’s time there should be a platform to allow students to review, rate, and see other student’s experiences with courses. We also thought that having grade distributions and other essential course information alongside reviews could tremedously benefit students looking for courses that fit their needs. With courseratings.ca, we’ve made this happen.

We believe this can aid students in the research of classes they're interested in and that these reviews can serve as a platform for quality, original, and trustworthy first person course reviews.

Sincerely,
Andrew Liu and Toorjo Mishra
